About Reha Beauty Clinic

Reha Beauty Clinic was created from a deep passion for beauty, skincare, and confidence. Founded in Srinagar, the clinic represents the dream of Reha — a professional beauty expert who believes beauty is both an art and a science.


Inspired by the natural beauty of Kashmir, Reha developed a strong dedication to skincare, makeup artistry, and professional beauty treatments. Her commitment to excellence and continuous learning helped her transform this passion into a trusted beauty brand.
